Our Story

Did you know?

Each of us generates about 6 tons of carbon emissions every single year

With NZ by Impacto, you have the opportunity to take meaningful action towards reducing your carbon footprint and preserving the planet through tree plantation without even having to leave your home.

Most of these emissions are inevitable owing to our basic living needs like food, clothing, housing, transportation, and electricity use. Though total elimination is unrealistic, reducing emissions to Net Zero is possible.
