Our Story

Did you know?

Each of us generates about 6 tons of carbon emissions every single year

Most of these emissions are inevitable owing to our basic living needs. We all need food to eat, clothes to wear, a home to live in, a commute to move around, electricity for practically everything, and the list goes on. While adopting sustainable alternatives in each of these areas is part of the solution, our emissions would never realistically be zero. But, what they can be is Net Zero!

With NZ by Impacto, you have the opportunity to take meaningful action towards reducing your carbon footprint and preserving the planet through tree plantation. Our subscription model makes it easy and convenient for you to offset your emissions by planting trees, without even having to leave your home.

Features

Still, have curiosi-tree about our plan? Here’s what’s in it for you!

Looking to drive positive impact in your name?

By subscribing to our plan, you're not only reducing your own carbon footprint but also helping to plant trees and combat deforestation

Want to fight against climate change?

At NZ by Impacto, we strive to work closely with non-profit organisations and other organisations that prioritise making a positive impact, with the goal of driving significant progress towards addressing climate change

Want to support a climate-tech startup?

Your monthly purchase of 25 dollars to NZ by Impacto can change the lives of those who make your life greener. As you invest in us, you fuel the engine that drives towards a cleaner and greener atmosphere. 80% of this will go in offsetting your carbon emissions, while 20% of this will be used to support Impacto

Wish to check up on your trees?

We provide E-tree certificates for trees that we plant for you. Your trees are stored in a tree bank with geotags, which you can access anytime to check up on your tree!

Seeking a chance to network with impact enthusiasts and environmentalists?

As a member of NZ by Impacto, you have the opportunity to connect with individuals who share your passion for making a positive impact and protecting the environment. The platform serves as a means of introducing you to other individuals and businesses that are committed to sustainability

Aiming to learn more about sustainability and positive impact?

NZ by Impacto strives to educate and empower its members by providing resources and information through seminars and webinars on how to lead a more sustainable lifestyle. The community allows you to share informative content about sustainability and social impact, enlightening all the members collectively

Searching for guidance from mentor experts?

At NZ by Impacto, we assist you in identifying and connecting with experienced mentors and experts who can guide and support you in achieving your sustainable goals. Whether you are seeking professional development or personal growth, we provide the resources to help you turn your vision for a more sustainable future into a reality

Ready for some exciting offers and exclusive benefits?

As a member of NZ by Impacto, you will have access to exclusive perks such as special discounts or promotions on sustainable products from Impacto, helping you gravitate towards a cleaner future.
