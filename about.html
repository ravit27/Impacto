Our Story

Did you know?

Each of us generates about 6 tons of carbon emissions every single year

Most of these emissions are inevitable owing to our basic living needs. We all need food to eat, clothes to wear, a home to live in, a commute to move around, electricity for practically everything, and the list goes on. While adopting sustainable alternatives in each of these areas is part of the solution, our emissions would never realistically be zero. But, what they can be is Net Zero!

With NZ by Impacto, you have the opportunity to take meaningful action towards reducing your carbon footprint and preserving the planet through tree plantation. Our subscription model makes it easy and convenient for you to offset your emissions by planting trees, without even having to leave your home.
